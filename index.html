The Importance of Digitization

Digitizing cultural heritage is a crucial step in:

  • Preserving the historical identity of nations
  • Enabling scientific research through digital means
  • Providing global access to cultural heritage
  • Digitally reconstructing destroyed monuments
  • Creating a sustainable digital archive for future generations
